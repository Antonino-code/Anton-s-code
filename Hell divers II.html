Helldivers II

Helldivers 2 is a cooperative third-person shooter developed by Arrowhead Game Studios and published by Sony Interactive Entertainment. It's the direct sequel to the 2015 top-down shooter, Helldivers.

Key Features:

  • Intense battles against invading bug creatures (Terminids) and mechanical robots (Automatons).
  • Use powerful 'stratagems' like cluster bombs, sentry guns, and supply pods.
  • Cooperative multiplayer for up to four players.
  • Optional cross-play between PlayStation 5 and Windows.
  • Dynamic gameplay with ongoing planetary liberation or defense.

Join the Helldivers and save humanity!
