Pacific Drive

"Pacific Drive" is a run-based driving survival adventure game set in an alternate version of the Pacific Northwest. Players must maintain their car and navigate through the Olympic Exclusion Zone, where anomalous activity abounds.

The game features various locations, hazards, and resources. Familiarize yourself with the map icons, including auto shops, gateways, highways, junctions, and more. Pay attention to energy sources (anchors) to unlock new recipes and fabricate blueprints.
