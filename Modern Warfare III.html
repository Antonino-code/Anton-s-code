Call of Duty: Modern Warfare III

In the direct sequel to the record-breaking Call of Duty®: Modern Warfare® II, Captain Price and Task Force 141 face off against the ultimate threat. The ultranationalist war criminal Vladimir Makarov is extending his grasp across the world, causing Task Force 141 to fight like never before.

Key Features:

  • Intense battles with different loadouts and paths through missions.
  • Open combat missions for more player choice.
  • Multiplayer with remastered and modernized maps from Modern Warfare 2 (2009).
  • Over 12 all-new core 6v6 maps for post-launch live seasons.
  • First-ever team-up against massive hordes of undead in the largest Call of Duty® Zombies map.

Get ready for an epic battle!
